Raise your arms to shoulder height. Then lower your arms palms down. (Figure 1.1)
Raise the right hand and toes, pivot 90 degrees to the right and place the foot flat on the floor. Pivot your left foot on the ball of the foot, turning the foot to a 45 degree angle and place it flat on the floor. Bend your right knee ·and straighten your left leg. Push your right hand straight ahead and your left palm down. (Figure 1.2)
