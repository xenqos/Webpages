40 heel kick right

turn body, kick with right heel
поворот пятка удар направо
повернуть тело, справа ударить пяткой

Shift your weight back to the left foot, and open and lower the arms in a scooping motion, continuing until your arms are crossed at the wrists at shoulder height, palms facing you, left hand inside. Kick with the right leg and open the arms as in the previous kicks. (Figure 48.1)
