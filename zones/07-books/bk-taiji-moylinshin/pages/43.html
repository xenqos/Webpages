43 turn heel kick left

turn body, kick with right heel
поворот пятка удар налево
повернуть тело, справа ударить пяткой
轉身蹬脚
zhuǎn shēn dēng jiǎo


Place the left foot down 45 degrees to the right (the pigeon-toed position), feet approximately shoulder width apart, and cross the arms at the wrists at shoulder height, palms inward. Tum hips and shoulders to the right and step “Pa Kua” with the right foot. Continue turning to the right and step with the left foot so that the feet are in the “pigeon-toed” position. Stand on the left leg, kick with the right foot and open the arms as in the other kicks. (Figure 51.1)
