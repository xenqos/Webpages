67 parry punch

deflect, parry, and punch
преграда удар
отклонить, преградить и ударить


Stand up (Figure 87.1) and step straight ahead with your left foot. Punch forward with your right hand, and push down with your left hand (as in “Brush Knee”) while shifting your weight forward and squaring your hips. Do not speed up the punch. (Figure 87.2)
