# Positions

## Button (BTN)
[!
Acts as Dealer
Shifts clockwise after each Hand
!]

## Under the Gun (UTG)
[!
First to act Preflop
Has least information
Gets maximum pressure
Relies on Hand strength
!]

## Under the Gun + 1 (UTG+1)
[!
Has minimal information
Must play relatively tight
!]

## Under the Gun + 2 (UTG+2)
[!
Has minimal information
Has disadvantageous position after flop
!]

## In Position (IP)
[!
Player acting last
Gathering more information
Enhanced calculation of odds
Leveraging free cards
!]

## Out of Position (OOP)
[!
Player acting first
!]
