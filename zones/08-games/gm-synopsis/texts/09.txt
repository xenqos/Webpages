###### Game

# Player Styles

## Loose
[!
Play many hands
Play weak hands
!]

## Passive
[!
Call frequently
Raise rarely
!]

## Tight
[!
Play strong hands
Fold weak hands
!]

## Aggressive
[!
Bet frequently
Raise frequently
Call rarely
!]

## TAG
Tight Aggressive

## LAG
Loose Aggressive

### Guidelines

if
  Table - Loose / Aggressive
then
  Play Tighter
endif

if
  Table - Tight / Passive
then
  Play Looser
endif
