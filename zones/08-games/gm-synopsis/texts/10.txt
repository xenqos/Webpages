###### Game

# Actions

## Check
[!
Pass Action
Not used in Preflop
Does not forfeit right to the pot
!]

## Fold
[!
Discard Hand
Forfeit right to the pot
!]

## Bet
[!
Make a wager
Place the wager into the pot
!]

## Call
[!
Match the current wager
!]

## Raise
[!
Increase current wager
At least double size
!]

## Re-raise
[!
Raise current raise
!]

## All in, Shove
[!
Bet all remaining chips
!]

## Raise Sequences
[|
|2bet|The second bet|first raise in a sequence |
|3bet|The third bet |second raise in a sequence|
|4bet|The fourth bet|third raise in a sequence |
|5bet|The fifth bet |fourth raise in a sequence|
|]
