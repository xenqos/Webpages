Rule 1
most verbs: -s

Rule 2
verbs ending in -o, -s, -ss, -sh, -ch, -tch, -x, -z: -es
 goes
 watches
 misses
 rushes
 mixes
 buzzes

Rule 3
verbs ending in consonant + y: -ies
 tries
 lies
