42 heel kick left

left kick with heel
пятка удар налево
влево ударить пяткой
左蹬脚
zuǒ dēng jiǎo


Stand up on your right leg as you push down and cross your arms at the wrists in front of you, scooping the arms up to shoulder height, palms facing inward. Kick to the left with left foot and open the arms as in the previous kicks. (Figure 50.1)
