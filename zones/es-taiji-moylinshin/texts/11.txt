Let your right arm swing down, back and upward to shoulder level (palm up) while extending your left arm forward. Step straight ahead with your left foot, keeping your weight on your right. Push straight ahead with your right palm, and down to your left knee with your left palm, while shifting your weight forward and squaring your hips. (Figure 13.1)
