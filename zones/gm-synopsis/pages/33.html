GTO

Pot Odds

Pot-Odds = Total-Pot / Hero-Bet

Example

Total-Pot = 20bb Pot + 10bb Bet = 30bb
Hero-Bet = 10bb
30bb / 10bb = 3
3:1

Pot Odds Break Even %

Pot Odds = 3:1 > 3 + 1 = 4
Break-Even-% = 100% / Pot odds added together)
100% / 4 = 25%

PostFlop Action

Outs-Probability > Pot-Odds-Break-Even-%
   Call

Outs-Probability < Pot-Odds-Break-Even-%
   Fold
