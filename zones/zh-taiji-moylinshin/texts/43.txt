# <small>43</small>&emsp;turn heel kick left
%[en2|turn body, kick with right heel]
%[ru1|поворот пятка удар налево]
%[ru2|повернуть тело, справа ударить пяткой]
<span class='zh1'>%[t3|轉]%[t1|身]%[t1|蹬]%[t3|脚]</span>
<span class='zh2'>zhuǎn shēn dēng jiǎo</span>

^[051.jpg]

Place the left foot down 45 degrees to the right (the pigeon-toed position), feet approximately shoulder width apart, and cross the arms at the wrists at shoulder height, palms inward. Tum hips and shoulders to the right and step &#8220;Pa Kua&#8221; with the right foot. Continue turning to the right and step with the left foot so that the feet are in the &#8220;pigeon-toed&#8221; position. Stand on the left leg, kick with the right foot and open the arms as in the other kicks. (Figure 51.1)
